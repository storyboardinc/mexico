Por Qué?

We’re a group of friends fortunate enough to have travelled and encountered a broad range of cultures and cuisine. We always love coming home, but sharing stories isn't the same as sharing experiences.

Mexico is our attempt to do that.

From the friendliness and hospitality to the exquisite coffee, cocktails, and tacos, Mexico is a country that lives with you. It’s a place where you get lost in flavours, laughter, and late night conversations.

Our hope is that we can carve out a little bit of that experience right here in Sarnia, with our families, friends, and friends to be.
